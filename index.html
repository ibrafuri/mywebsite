What does the Kia company do?

Kia is a Korean auto manufacturer that, along with Genesis, is an offshoot of parent company Hyundai. Kia makes sporty cars like the Stinger and midsize sedans like the K5. Kia SUVs range from small to full-size and include the subcompact Seltos, the compact Sportage, and the midsize Sorento and Telluride.

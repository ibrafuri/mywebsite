Kia

Home of the Greatest minds new cars

Kia is a Korean auto manufacturer that, along with Genesis, is an offshoot of parent company Hyundai. Kia makes sporty cars like the Stinger and midsize sedans like the K5. Kia SUVs range from small to full-size and include the subcompact Seltos, the compact Sportage, and the midsize Sorento and Telluride.

Manufacturing and sales of motor vehicles Kia Motors Corp. engages in the manufacturing and sales of motor vehicles. It operates through the following segment: Passenger Cars, Recreational Vehicles and Commercial Vehicles. It is also involved in leasing vehicles, providing vehicle maintenance services and automobile Parts.

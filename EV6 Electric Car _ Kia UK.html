EV powertrains

All power to you

 

Two electric powertrain variants — rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive – offer different range and performance characteristics.

RWD

Combined with the 77.4 kWh battery pack, this rear-wheel drive can take you up to 328 miles on one charge. Ultra-fast charge from 10 to 80% in only 18 minutes, so you can stay on the road for longer.

  • Battery pack: 77.4 kWh
  • Drivetrain: Rear-wheel drive
  • Range: Up to 328 miles
  • Fast charging: 10-80% in 18 minutes
  • 0-62 mph: 7.3 seconds
  • Power: 226 bhp (168kW)
  • Top speed: 114 mph
  • Towing capacity: Up to 1,600 kg

AWD

The EV6’s e-AWD system provides optimised efficiency together with supreme grip and performance.

  • Battery pack: 77.4 kWh
  • Drivetrain: All-wheel drive
  • Range: Up to 314 miles
  • Fast charging: 10-80% in 18 minutes
  • 0-62 mph: 5.2 seconds
  • Power: 321 bhp (239 kW)
  • Top speed: 116 mph
  • Towing capacity: Up to 1,600 kg

E-GMP

A new platform, and a new world of opportunities

 

Meet the future of electric mobility. The Electric-Global Modular Platform (E-GMP) is a new dedicated platform created to deliver new standards of performance and design. Allowing for more cabin space, it also makes provision for an interior that's all about inspiring your senses.

  • Vehicle-to-Load
  • Multi-speed charging
  • Designed for the future
  • A more spacious cabin
  • Built for better performance

  • A portable power supply

    Charge your appliances wherever you are. Vehicle-to-Load (V2L) is a function that allows you to use your EV6 as a portable power supply. Supplying you with up to 3.6kW of electricity, it can power everything from camping equipment, movie projectors, and even charge other electric vehicles.

  • Flexible charging

    Thanks to the EV6's 800 V high-speed charging, you can charge the battery from 10 to 80% in just 18 minutes. From this, you'll get 62 miles of range in just 4.5 minutes. And, with the multi-charging system, you can use both 800 V high-speed charging and the more mainstream 400 V fast charging.

    With 400 V charging, the EV6 uses the electric motor and inverter to convert the 400 V current from the charger into 800 V. All this happens smoothly and intuitively, allowing you to get the optimum charging speed. Find out more about electric car charging

  • Revolutionising vehicle proportions

    The foundation of a better form factor. Thanks to the Electric-Global Modular Platform, the EV6 is able to change what's possible in terms of vehicle proportions. The longer wheelbase, larger wheels, shorter front and rear overhangs, allow for a truly modern use of space — ready to revolutionise the future of driving.

  • More space to relax in

    The EV6 introduces an optimised flat cabin floor. This maximises interior space for you and your passengers. The interior space provides ample legroom and a new, smart cockpit design.

  • A revolution in layout

    A radically different approach to engineering. The EV6 offers improved cornering performance and driving stability at high speed — with better weight distribution between front and rear, and a lower centre of gravity.

    Its five-link suspension system, as well as the world's first mass-produced integrated drive axle (IDA), gives you a driving experence with a new level of comfort.

Take a fresh look at electric

Powerful and sporty

Designed to be daring. The Kia EV6 is a fully electric crossover that is capable of 0-62 mph in 3.5 seconds and a top speed of 161 mph.

A space to get inspired in

A cabin developed with your comfort in mind. A flat floor supports premium relaxation seats (available on GT-Line and GT-Line S) and enough space for all the things you want to bring along for the ride.

Safety first

The Kia EV6 is simply loaded with safety features. For example, Forward Collision-avoidance Assist gives you a second pair of eyes, always on the lookout for the potential for immediate collision. Lane Keep Assist keeps you from drifting out of your lane due to sleepiness or inattention.

Advanced pedal technology

Introducing the I-PEDAL — a feature that lets you accelerate, decelerate and fully stop via a unique throttle pedal. The EV6 comes with a regenerative braking system that harnesses energy generated by deceleration, recycling it back into the battery to improve range and efficiency.

Connected infotainment

 

The EV6 onboard infotainment system. An easy way to visualise your journey in detail — and read the road in real-time. All your information is displayed on two striking 12.3" curved displays for complete navigational oversight.

  • Kia Connect
  • Augmented Reality Head-up Display
  • The Meridian Premium Sound System
  • Panoramic Dual 12.3" Curved Displays

  • Allow connected

    Kia Connect opens your EV6 to the power of the internet. It powers your navigation system with live and predictive traffic information for the best route recommendations and journey time estimates. It also includes a connected Points-of-Interest directory, with public charging stations and their availability status. You can interact with your EV6 – like scheduling charging sessions – by pairing it with the Kia Connect app.

  • Available on GT-Line S models and above.

    AR always in view

    An advanced function that projects key information directly onto your windscreen. This means navigation guidance, lane safety, and more, are always right in your eyeline. A great way to keep your focus on the road so you reach your destination safely.

  • Available on GT-Line S models and above

    Wired for sound

    Immerse yourself and your occupants in a rich audio landscape. The Meridian premium sound system delivers rich acoustics whether you're listening to a podcast, your favourite artist or navigation instructions.

  • A view to a thrill

    A drive with real vision. With the immersive Panoramic dual 12.3" curved displays, you're able to select exactly what you need to see, simply and intuitively. Complete control right at your fingertips.

Keeping you, and your loved ones, safe

 

Stay in safe hands, wherever you are on your journey. The EV6 provides you with an extensive array of safety features.

  • Intelligent Front Lighting System
  • Highway Driving Assist
  • Highway Driving with Lane Change Assist
  • Blind-Spot Collision Avoidance Assist
  • Rear Cross-Traffic Avoidance Assist
  • Remote Smart Parking Assist
  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist
  • Navigation-Based Smart Cruise Control

  • Better visibility, safer drives

    Safety, illuminated. With Intelligent Front Lighting your EV6 senses the vehicles around you, and automatically controls its high beam function — providing you with the best possible visibility without blinding other road users.

  •

    Safety at a distance

    Highway Driving Assist is available when on the motorway. When engaged, it automatically adapts your target speed to the limit in force while maintaining a safe distance from any vehicles ahead. It also helps you stay centred in your lane so your long journeys are as relaxing as possible.

  •

    Available on GT-Line S models and above

    Lane change made easy

    Highway Driving Assist with Lane Change is available when on the motorway. When engaged, it automatically adapts your target speed to the limit in force while maintaining a safe distance from any vehicles ahead. It also helps you stay centred in your lane and can operate lane changes automatically. Just activate your indicator and EV6 will change lane if conditions allow.

    A super-advanced smart feature that alerts you to vehicles in your blind spot or those approaching fast to overtake you. When the system detects a risk of collision, it'll intuitively assist you with evasive steering and braking.

    Reverse safely

    Back out of a parking space or driveway simply and easily. The EV6's Rear Cross-traffic Avoidance system gives you a handy heads-up if cars, or pedestrians, are passing behind you. And, if there's a risk of a collision, the system automatically brakes.
